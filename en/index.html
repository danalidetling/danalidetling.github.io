DANALI DETLING

Architecture and Construction

Biography

Danalí Detling was born in Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina. After a few years her family moved to Santiago del Estero where she grew up and obtained the title of Art Teacher. She continued her studies at the Faculty of Architecture and Urbanism in the city of Tucumán obtaining the title of Architect. After the opening of her studio in 2018, her start was with design, planimetric representations and renders. In 2019 she founded Rustic Style in Santiago del Estero and there she works as an architect and builder. Its main function is the manufacture of furniture and decorative items that complement the design and construction of the architectural studio.

Mission

Carry out architectural, construction and decoration design projects that are functional, aesthetic and of quality, interpreting the needs and expectations of the client.

Vision

Being an architecture studio that provides a complete process, from design, construction and decoration. Strengthen ties with studies from different countries to create joint projects.

Services

Design and Construction

Residential and commercial architecture projects from design to execution
